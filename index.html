Stones Of Gaia


Stones Of Gaia is a simple addictive puzzle game wrapped in a mystery and beautiful 3D world.
Your job is moving colored stones, with your fingers, in 7x5 size gridded field.
Each stone must reach to its GOAL position!
But the stones are moving synchronized…
Are you ready to play? Download and start solving puzzles now!
